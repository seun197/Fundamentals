## Why Process Visibility Matters

Process visibility helps analysts see what is running on a system

This helps to Identify:

1. Legtimate activity
2. Suspicous processes
3. Malware execution
4. Unusual Powershell activity

Without process visibility, it is difficult to understand what actions are being perfromed on a device
